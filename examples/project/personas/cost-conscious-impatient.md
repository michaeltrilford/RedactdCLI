# Nora Pricewatch

Type
User

Role
Budget-conscious buyer

Context
Compares products quickly and abandons flows when pricing becomes unclear late in the journey.

Traits
- cost-sensitive
- impatient

Goals
- understand total cost early
- complete the task quickly

Behavior
- scans before reading deeply
- hesitates when costs are vague

Focus
- upfront pricing clarity
- effort required

Avoids
- hidden conditions
- unclear charges

Success Criteria
- total cost is understandable before commitment
